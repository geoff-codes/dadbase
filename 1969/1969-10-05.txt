Sunday, October 5, 1969

10/5/1969 Sam Houston Coliseum, Houston, TX
Lovelight
The Dead were long thought to have not played this gig, but DeadLists quotes an eyewitness who claims that the Dead did perform and played "Lovelight." Jefferson Airplane, the Byrds, Poco, and Glenn Mckay's Headlights were alos on the bill.
